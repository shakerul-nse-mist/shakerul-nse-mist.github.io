About Md Shakerul Islam:

 

I, Md Shakerul Islam, was born at the district of Pabna located in the north side of Bangladesh. I have grown up in a middle-class family. My father is a simple businessman who is the only earning member of our family. My mother is a house wife. I have started my educational career at Govt. Laboratory High School at the age of 6. In the long journey, I have participated a lot of extracurricular activities. Before appearing secondary school certificate exam, I have participated Math Olympiad, Science Olympiad, Project Fest and many more competitions. I was an active member quiz club of Govt. Laboratory High School. I have nominated for the Expo-Asia project fest arranged by Ministry of Science and Technology, Bangladesh at the year 2009. After passing the secondary school certificate exam with cumulative CGPA of 5.0 I have admitted to Dhaka College. During two-year courses I was an active member of Dhaka College Science Club and have participated a number of science fest at national level. For my effort to the club I have obtained position at project management in its administration panel. I have obtained cumulative CGPA of 5.0 in my Higher secondary certificate exam. After that I have admitted to Military Institute of Science and Technology, one of the leading educational institute in Bangladesh under Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP). I have earned my degree at Bachelor of Science in Nuclear Science and Engineering at 2020 by successfully completing 160 credit hours. During the four years of study I have successfully completed 5 government funding project and was able to publish four research paper based on those study. The projects are mainly focused in thermal hydraulics, nuclear electronics and nuclear medical physics. Besides my core study I have also managed to learn basic knowledge in different programming languages. I have also completed training on Personal Computer Transient Analysis (PCTRAN) software which is basically focused on accident analysis. In the future my vision is to carry out my research work in order to participate in the technological enhancement of the world.
